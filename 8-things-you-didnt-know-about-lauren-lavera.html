Lauren LaVera plays the pivotal character Sienna in Damien Leone’s new horror sequel Terrifier 2, which he also wrote and directed. The actress has wowed the audience with her spectacular on-screen performances.

So it’s time to learn a little bit more about this lovely lady by reading the eight unknown facts that will pique your interest.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Lauren LaVera

1. 19+ Years Of Martial Arts Experience

Lauren has 19+ years of martial arts expertise, including Taekwon-Do, Jiu Jitsu, Kun Khmer, Wushu, and Muay Thai, boxing, kickboxing, weapon, and firearms training. [1]

2. Trained Ballet Dancer

When it comes to her skills, the talented actress has a lot to show. She is a trained ballet dancer who also knows how to ball dance. Lauren is a multi-talented personality who is now showing her talents on TV.

3. Can Speak Italian

We all know that it’s quite easy to speak your mother tongue. But we all find it difficult to speak any other language. But Lauren seems quite fluent in speaking Italian.

4. Love Horror Movies

Lauren told in one of her interviews that she actually loves watching horror movies with her friends. Even her friends recommended LaVera to watch the film. [2]

5. Never Thought Of Being A Right Fit For Terrifier 2

The actress also stated that she was unsure if she was the proper match for the character of Sienna in Terrifier 2. She even suggests that some of her other actor pals audition for the part. Fortunately, this character was tailor-made for her, and the director approached LaVera to perform it.

6. Performed Her Own Stunts

Because the actress is a strong martial artist, she likes to perform her own stunts in Terrifier 2. She also mentioned that doing it on her own was easier for her. [3]

7. Enjoys Her Time Outside In Nature

The actress is often seen adoring the beauty of nature in many of her posts on Instagram. So we can easily conclude that she is a nature lover who loves to spend her time outside.

8. Active On Social Platforms

Lauren never misses a chance to update her fans with her new projects. She is quite active on her social accounts and keeps her fans updated with her work projects.
