Meet Taylor Swift at midnight. The singer set watches off at the 2024 Grammys with her Schiaparelli white gown and matte black gloves. Although, most fans were zoned in on her unique Lorraine Schwartz choker and layered necklaces. The luxury piece features a clock strung together by elegant black diamonds. Per GQ, a rep for Schwartz said it’s a “custom-made vintage watch that was turned into a choker.” It carries 300 carats of Lorraine Schwartz Diamonds.

Swift paired the look with a dark red lip (obviously) and pearl earrings. Fans were split on the singer’s hairstyle but soon forgot about the divided opinions after her big announcement following a win for best pop vocal album.
