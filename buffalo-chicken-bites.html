    Buffalo Chicken Bites

    Easy Buffalo Chicken Bites are packed full of flavor! Cheesy and delicious, this is a great game-day appetizer! 

    Course Appetizer Cuisine American Keyword buffalo chicken bites Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 15 minutes minutes Servings 22 Calories 51kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 4 ounces cream cheese I used light
    • 1 tbsp red pepper sauce
    • 10 oz RO*TEL® Original Diced Tomatoes & Green Chilies drained well
    • 5 oz chicken breast finely diced, can you canned or rotisserie chicken
    • 3/4 cup mozzarella cheese shredded
    • 22 frozen mini Phyllo (fillo) cups from 2 boxes, 1.9 oz each

    Instructions

    • Preheat oven to 350°F.

    • Microwave cream cheese and red pepper sauce in a medium bowl for 30 seconds on high. Stir until smooth.

    • Add drained tomatoes, chicken and shredded cheese to the cream cheese mixture and stir to combine.

    • Divide mixture evenly between fillo shells; place in large shallow baking pan.

    • Bake 15 minutes or until cheese melts.

    • Serve immediately.
