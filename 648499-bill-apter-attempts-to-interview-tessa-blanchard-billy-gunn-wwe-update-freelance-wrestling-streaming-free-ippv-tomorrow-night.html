Billy Gunn-WWE Performance Center Update

Earlier today WrestleZone Daily featured Great Lake Championship Wrestling Owner Dave Herro as it’s featured guest. Dave was on the show to discuss GLCW’s biggest show of the year this Saturday, Blizzard Brawl, as well as his Monster’s Ball match on the show with TNA Superstar Abyss. We will have transcribed quotes from what Dave had to say about that tomorrow on the site.

During Dave’s appearance I brought up that one of the featured talents for Blizzard Brawl, Chyna, recently was seen in a photo alongside Billy Gunn.

When I asked Dave if he was surprised to see Billy posing with Chyna so soon after his release from the WWE he casually confirmed the speculation that Billy Gunn would be returning in the future as a trainer to WWE NXT. Below is the full transcript of his quote as well as the full audio.

DH: First of all, Billy has been a friend of mine for a long time. He’s had his set backs. If he wouldn’t have been tested by the World Bodybuilding or whatever it was… nobody would have known anything. It’s just unfortunate. He will be back as an NXT trainer soon. Joanie and Billy go way back. It’s unfortunate the way she left the company and how things happened for her as she was gone from the company. It’s still a brotherhood. It’s still a big family. At the end of the day, anyone who is involved in the world of pro wrestling you are kind of a misfit to begin with. The Island of Wrestling is The Island of Misfit Toys. We can all get mad at each other or whatever else but we are always going to be friends and family and wish the best for everybody.
