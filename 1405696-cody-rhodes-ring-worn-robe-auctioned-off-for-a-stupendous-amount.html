The Stamford-based promotion recently announced that they would auction off a Cody Rhodes robe. The robe was the one that The American Nightmare wore at the 2023 Night of Champions Premium Live Event. It is also signed by Rhodes.

The robe was successfully auctioned off, selling for an impressive $46,700. The American Nightmare also revealed that all the profits will be used for a great cause. He mentioned that the proceedings will go to Connor’s Cure and The V Foundation.

“Amazing 46,700 dollars $46.7k for this robe! All benefits going to Connor’s Cure & @TheVFoundation Thank you to all who donated,” Cody Rhodes tweeted.

Last night on Raw, The American Nightmare once again had a warm welcome from the WWE Universe in Salt Lake City. He took to Twitter to thank the fans for their support.

The American Nightmare had a match against NXT North American Champion Dominik Mysterio. Despite having Finn Balor and Damian Priest in Dirty Dom’s corner, Cody Rhodes managed to earn the victory.

After the match, Judgment Day looked to attack Rhodes but Kevin Owens and Sami Zayn rushed in to help Rhodes. This resulted in Judgment Day retreating. Rhodes and Owens had a few exchanges of words. The Prizefighter wanted to know the real reason why Rhodes brought Jey Uso to Monday Night Raw. The American Nightmare revealed the real reason was because the fans love Mr. Main Event and he felt Jey deserved a second chance.
